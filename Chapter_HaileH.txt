The Danger of Biting on a Phish
Numerous cyber-attacks ought to be known by all managers and employees at Sifers-Grayson. While phishing is among the common cyber-attacks, it remains to be the most annoying threat we can ever face. Most people acknowledge what it is and how it occurs but still, get caught out (Fruhlinger, 2020). On this note, we can start by asking ourselves what phishing is. Phishing can be described as a fraudulent effort to acquire sensitive data or information, including credit card information, passwords, and usernames, or other crucial information, by impersonating themselves in a digital platform as a genuine entity.
